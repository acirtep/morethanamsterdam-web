About Discover The Netherlands by Train

What is this?

Discover The Netherlands by Train is a web application that helps you plan day trips across the Netherlands using the extensive Dutch railway network. Instead of just visiting Amsterdam, explore the diverse cities, towns, and attractions that the Netherlands has to offer - all accessible by train.

How it works

Our tool allows you to:

  • Plan your journey: Select departure and arrival stations from hundreds of train stations across the Netherlands
  • Optimize your time: Set departure and arrival times to make the most of your day trip
  • Find layover opportunities: Discover interesting stops along your route with customizable layover durations
  • Explore by day: Plan trips for different days of the week, taking advantage of varying train schedules

Why travel by train in the Netherlands?

The Netherlands has one of the most efficient and comprehensive railway networks in Europe:

  • Extensive coverage: Trains reach almost every corner of the country
  • Frequent services: Regular departures throughout the day
  • Environmental friendly: Sustainable way to explore the country
  • Convenient: No parking hassles or traffic concerns
  • Scenic routes: Enjoy the Dutch countryside from your window seat

About the data

This application uses official train schedule data and station information to provide accurate trip planning. The data includes:

  • Train stations across the Netherlands with precise locations
  • Service schedules for different days of the week
  • Connection information for planning multi-leg journeys
